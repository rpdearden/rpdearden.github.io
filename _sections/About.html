I am a PhD student at Imperial College London, Silwood Park Campus, in Martin Brazeau's research group. My PhD research focuses on the early evolution of the cartilaginous fishes - the group comprising living sharks, rays, and chimaeras. I do this particularly using tomographic and phylogenetic techniques More broadly I have an interest in macroevolutionary processes from my Masters work, particularly disparity and character rate analyses. Outside fossil fishes my pursuits of choice include cooking (bread/pizza specialist), reading (more or less anything), gaming (mainly grand strategy), and history (of most flavours).
