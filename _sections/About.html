--- title: About Me icon: fa-user order: 2 --- I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Muséum national d'histoire naturelle, Paris. I am based in the Centre de recherche en paléontologie (CR2P), with Alan Pradel. My research focuses on the early evolution of the cartilaginous fishes - the group of animals which today comprises living sharks, rays, and chimaeras. I do this particularly using tomographic and phylogenetic techniques. More broadly I have an interest in macroevolutionary processes from my Masters work, particularly disparity and character rate analyses. Outside fossil fishes my pursuits of choice include cooking (bread/pizza specialist), reading (particularly history/sci-fi), gaming (mainly grand strategy).
